Our Story

The journey of Kashyap International

Crafting Quality. Exporting Excellence.

Kashyap International is a globally recognized name in the natural stone industry, specializing in the export of premium granite sourced from the finest quarries in India. With years of experience and a strong focus on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have earned the trust of clients across the world—from large construction firms to boutique design studios.

Founded in 2020, our journey began with a vision to take the natural beauty of Indian granite to international markets. Today, we proudly serve customers in North America, Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and Africa, offering a wide range of granite products tailored to diverse architectural and design needs.

Our Vision

To be a globally respected granite exporter known for quality, integrity, and customer-first service.
